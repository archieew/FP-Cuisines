PASTIL

Here are the Instructions how to make pastil

Ingredients

Here are the Instructions and step by step making of the pastil, It is easy to make and delicious. Try and follow this recipe now.

1. 1/2 kg of chicken (depends on how many serving you are going to serve) 1. Heat the banana leaves indirectly over your stove to avoid brittleness.
2. 1 cup of chicken broth or 1 buoillion cube 2. Cook your rice in a pot or in a rice cooker and set aside.
3. 1/4 cup of dark soy sauce (silver swan) or any soy sauce of your choice 3. Boil the chicken in a pot until tender. Reserve the broth.
4. 1 tsp white sugar 4. Shred the cooked chicken into small strips.
5. 3 cloves of garlic 5. Heat your wok and pour in cooking oil. Saute the garlic and onion until fragrant. Add the shredded chicken and simmer for minutes. Add a cup of chicken broth and simmer until the liquid evaporates then add a pinch of ground black pepper.
6. 1 medium onion 6. Add the dark soy sauce and simmer for 15 minutes. Pour the oyster sauce and 1 tsp sugar. Add water or broth if needed according to your taste preference.
7. a pinch of ground black pepper 7. Once the liquid subsides, Turn off the heat and transfer to serving bowl.
8. 1 tbsp of cooking oil/td> 8. Place the banana leaf in a clean kitchen table. Brush a little oil on the leaf. Put half cup of rice on the leaf and add 1 tbsp of the chicken filling. Wrap neatly and serve.
9. 20 pcs of banana leaves (semi-rectangular cut)
10. 10 cups of cooked white rice

If you have leftover fish, chicken or beef from yesterday’s meal, you can easily prepare pastil. Pastil is made of boiled rice and in the middle topped with ‘kagikit’ – sautéed shredded meat of chicken, beef, or fish that is wrapped with a wilted banana leaf. It can be prepared with only shredded beef, chicken, fish or you can have it all.

The more ingredients, the tastier it is. But chicken and fish pastil are the famous here and in Maguindanao.
